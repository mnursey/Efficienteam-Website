Our Client's project

The clients we are working for are a driven group of individuals working to enhance the system at the McKinnon Building pool at The University of Victoria in BC. These clients consist of two project managers who have planned a system in order to improve the current paper based scheduling system for the facility employees. This organization aims to provide their employees with an easy to use and efficient system in order to provide them with the schedules they desire.

Our organization consists of a six member team. Bob Liu, Jeet Bains, Eric Skelton, Mitchell Nursey, Mitchell Quak, and Isaac Waite. These members hold the positions of project managers, developers, analysts, and programers. Our company is known for its experienced employees, good customer service and excellent project development.
